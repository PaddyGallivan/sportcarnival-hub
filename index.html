How It Works

From setup to trophy — in minutes.

SportCarnival handles the paperwork so you can focus on running a great day. Four simple steps gets you from nothing to a fully drawn carnival.

1

Set Up Your Carnival

Choose your carnival type, set the date, configure your house colours and age groups. Takes under 5 minutes.

›
2

Enter Athletes

Import your student list or enter athletes manually. Assign events, age groups and houses.

›
3

Draw the Heats

SportCarnival automatically generates heat draws based on entries. Print or share directly. No spreadsheets required.

›
4

Record Results Live

Enter results on the day — points auto-calculate, leaderboards update instantly and records are flagged automatically.

Features

Everything a PE teacher actually needs.

No bloated software. No training required. Just the tools that make carnival day easier.

📋

Student Entry Management

Import or manually add athletes, assign events and enforce entry limits per student.

🔀

Automatic Heat Draw

Heats drawn instantly based on entries. Balanced lanes, correct age groups, no manual sorting.

📊

Live Results Sheet

Enter results as events finish. Points tally in real time. No paper totalling required.

🏠

House Points Tracker

Configurable points for 1st, 2nd, 3rd. House leaderboard updates live throughout the day.

🖨️

Printable Programs

Generate a full heat draw program for officials and parents with one click. Print-ready PDF.

🏅

Records & PBs

Automatically flags school records and personal bests as results are entered.

📤

District Nomination Export

One-click export of your top performers in the format required for district nomination.

📱

Works on Any Device

Run it on your phone, tablet or laptop on the day. No app to install. Just open and go.

🔁

Reusable Year to Year

Clone last year's carnival setup, update the students, and you're done. Faster every year.
