How this demo works

This is what your carnival page looks like. Students are entered once at the start of the term (CSV, spreadsheet, or Compass export). On carnival day, timers tap results on their phones and this page updates live. House points auto-tally. School and district records flag automatically.

Privacy: This public view shows first name + last initial + school only. DOB never appears. Staff signed in to their own school see full names internally. Full privacy spec on sportportal.com.au.

Real district? Real schools? On the live platform, the top 4 per event auto-nominate to the district carnival — no extra paperwork.
